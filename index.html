Blaze is a National Level College Sports Festival hosted by NMIMS ASMSOC Sports Club. It attracts more than 50 teams from across the country to compete for the championship and a massive footfall of 4000+ sports enthusiasts over 3 days of the fest. Blaze ventured into Football and later added Basketball after the success of its first edition. It is held at one of the most prestigious grounds in Mumbai with top notch facilities; The Jamnabai Narsee Playground. We ensure the maximum level of professionalism in terms of accommodation, amenities at the ground, proper equipment, trained professionals for officiating the matches, medical facilities and refreshments for our national as well as international participants. Within four years of its incorporation, Blaze has had affiliation with the likes of Star Sports, Mumbai City FC, ONGC, LIC, Redbull and many more. The ‘festive’ outlook of Blaze makes it memorable for both the participants and the organising team. Blaze'20 took place in the month of January and had an eclectic blend of amazing sportsmen from all over the country, enthusiastic sponsors and dedicated management.

The NMIMS ASMSOC Sports Club is the official organizer and coordinator for all sporting activities of NMIMS University’s School of Commerce. Since its commencement in 2014 with 4 sports events and a team of 30 members, the club has been expanding its operations every year and currently hosts 10 events in a year with a member strength of over 200 people. The club provides the students of NMIMS a platform to stay connected to sports by helping them organize and manage various intra-college sports events throughout the year. We also manage the college sports teams and coordinate their affairs with different sports fests and tournaments. The Sports Club hosts a National Level Sports Fest called Blaze which attracts a participation of over 600 players from around the country.
